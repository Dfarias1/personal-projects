Let's Play Rock, Paper, Scissors!

THANKS FOR PLAYING

User Stories

  • As a visitor, I expect to be asked to play a game of Rock, Paper, Scissors.
  • As a visitor, I expect the program to end if I click cancel.
  • As a visitor, when I click OK to play, I will be prompted to select a choice.
  • As a visitor, after I input my choice, I expect to be alerted of my choice, and the choice of the NPC.
  • As a visitor, I expect to see the correct result of the game.
  • As a visitor, I expect to be asked to play again.

New Features

  • The user will be asked to play again at the end of every game.
  • Strings will be converted to one case to accurately compare string values.
